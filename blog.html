Quick Tips

Essential Moving Advice

Start Early

Begin planning your move at least 8 weeks in advance. Early preparation reduces stress, secures better removal company availability, and gives you time to declutter and organize systematically.

Label Everything

Clearly label all boxes with contents and destination rooms. Use color-coded systems for different rooms and mark fragile items prominently to ensure careful handling during transit.

Pack Smart

Use proper packing materials, wrap fragile items individually, and pack heavy items in small boxes. Don't overload boxes and always pack books flat to prevent spine damage.

Create Essentials Box

Pack a first-day essentials box with toiletries, change of clothes, important documents, phone chargers, and basic kitchen items. Keep this box with you during the move.

Notify Important Parties

Update your address with banks, utilities, DVLA, electoral register, doctors, dentists, and subscription services. Arrange mail forwarding with Royal Mail for peace of mind.

Take Photos

Photograph furniture assembly, electronic connections, and room layouts before dismantling. These reference images make reassembly in your new home much easier.
