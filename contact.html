Frequently Asked Questions

How much do removal services cost?

Costs vary based on property size, distance, and services required. Man & van from ¬£45/hr, house removals from ¬£85/hr, with fixed-price quotes available. Request a free quote for accurate pricing.

Are you fully insured?

Yes, we carry comprehensive public liability insurance covering all your belongings during transit and storage. Additional goods-in-transit insurance is included with every move at no extra cost.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we supply all necessary packing materials including boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, and furniture blankets. Materials can be provided in advance or as part of our full packing service.

Can you move on short notice?

Yes, we offer same-day and next-day removal services subject to availability. Contact us as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate urgent moves.

What areas do you cover?

We serve all Greater London boroughs including London, Salford, Bolton, Bury, Oldham, Rochdale, Stockport, Tameside, Trafford, and Wigan. Long-distance moves available UK-wide.

Do you offer storage solutions?

Yes, we provide secure, climate-controlled storage facilities with flexible rental periods. Perfect for temporary storage during house sales, renovations, or downsizing. 24/7 security and individual unit access.
